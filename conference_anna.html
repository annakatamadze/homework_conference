Mental Health and Wellness Conference

This conference can address the growing concerns around mental health and well-being, and explore ways to promote positive mental health practices and policies.

The main goal of this conference would be to address the growing concerns around mental health and well-being, and to explore ways to promote positive mental health practices and policies. The conference could bring together mental health professionals, researchers, advocates, policymakers, and other stakeholders to share their expertise and experiences.
